Apple Ecosystem

The power of Apple products isn't just in what they do—it's in how they work together. Your iPhone, Mac, iPad, and Apple Watch are designed to connect seamlessly, creating an ecosystem where everything just works.

Continuity

Start an email on your iPhone and finish it on your Mac. Copy text on your iPad and paste it on your Mac. With Continuity, everything flows seamlessly across your devices.

AirDrop

Quickly share photos, documents, and files between your iPhone, iPad, and Mac with AirDrop. No wires, no hassle, just simple sharing.

Handoff

Pick up where you left off. Handoff lets you continue what you were doing across different devices, making switching from one device to another effortless.
